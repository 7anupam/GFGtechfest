Ergonomics for Office Workers

Proper ergonomics in your workspace can greatly reduce physical strain and discomfort. Here are some tips to optimize your desk setup:

1. Desk and Chair Setup

Ensure that your desk and chair are adjusted to fit your body. Your chair should support your lower back, and your desk should be at a height where your elbows are at a 90-degree angle.

2. Monitor Position

Your monitor should be at eye level to avoid straining your neck. The top of the screen should be about eye level and the screen should be about an arm's length away from you.
