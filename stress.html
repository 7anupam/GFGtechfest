Managing Stress at Work

Stress is a common issue in the workplace, but effective management strategies can improve your mental well-being. Here are some techniques to help you handle stress:

1. Identify Stressors

Keep track of what causes your stress and how you respond to it. Understanding your stressors is the first step in managing them effectively.

2. Practice Mindfulness

Engage in mindfulness exercises to stay grounded. Simple techniques such as deep breathing and meditation can help reduce stress levels.
